Dynamax is a unique phenomenon where Pokémon grow significantly larger, surrounded by a mysterious red glow and swirling clouds. Pokémon with the ability to Dynamax can be encountered, battled, and caught through Max Battles.

Dynamax Pokémon can be found in the wild at Power Spots throughout the map. Partner with your own Dynamax Pokémon in order to defeat Power Spot Bosses! Select one of your Dynamax Pokémon and use Candy and Max Particles to unlock Max Moves, and level up the moves for stronger effects!
